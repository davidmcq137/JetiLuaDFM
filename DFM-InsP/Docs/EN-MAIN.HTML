DFM-InsP Help

DFM-InsP is the companion app to the Instrument Panel Creator website available at www.jwtiluadfm.app/gauges.

  • Help screens such as this one will be available on some screens on Softkey 1 with the help icon, and on all screens with the Menu button on the TX.

  • You start on the website by modifying one of the example panels, or starting from scratch with the Empty panel. You can create as many panels as you like.

  • When you are ready to transfer the panels and the app to the transmitter, you press the Create app source button which will give you a link to paste into the Jeti studio app sources tab available under the Configuration menu.
