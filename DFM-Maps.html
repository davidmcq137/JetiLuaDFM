DFM-Maps

GPS Maps Display

Displays the model's flightpath on the TX screen overlaid on Google maps images of your flying field. Automatically zooms to fit flightpath.

You can set up multiple circular or polygonal no-fly zones which can be no-fly inside or no-fly outside. There is a warning when approaching a no-fly zone, and an audio and tactile (stick shaker) alert available when crossing into a no-fly zone

The model's flight path history is available up to 600 points and this trail can be color-coded with various parameters of interest such as antenna signal, altitude, or speed

There is a companion website that creates map sets for your flying fields and creates a Jeti Studio "App store" with your fields and your maps for easy downloads to your transmitter

We also support creation of a GPS Triangle race "track" and navigation of your model around the track by voice command along with Triangle race scorekeeping

Usage

  1. Go to www.jetiluadfm.app to create your fields and maps
  2. Open Jeti Studio and paste in your personal App Store URL from the website into File/Configuration/App sources
  3. Plug your transmitter into the computer via USB
  4. UseJeti Studio Lua App Manager to download the App, along with your maps
  5. This app requires Transmitter update 5.03 or newer
